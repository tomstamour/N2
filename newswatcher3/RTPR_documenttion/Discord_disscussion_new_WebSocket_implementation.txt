@everyone Hey everyone,

I have some news to share — some not great, but hopefully the workaround makes it manageable.

After a review with our licensing provider, I've been informed that articles can only be displayed to end users directly — they can't be delivered through an API or WebSocket in raw form. Because of this, I'll be shutting down the direct article API and the article WebSocket at the end of the month.

Here's the workaround I've built:

Over the last two weeks I've completely revamped the product around this constraint. If you head to rtpr.io/wire and log in, you'll see the new live feed — articles show up in real time just like before, just behind the dashboard.

For Pro users, the bigger addition is filter rules and a new alert WebSocket. You define the rules (ticker, keywords, sources, etc.), and when an article matches, a lightweight notification gets pushed to your WebSocket. That notification includes a URL pointing to the article. You can then curl that URL with your API key to pull the full article content — the whole round trip has been running about 100ms slower than the old direct flow in my own testing, though obviously that can vary.

I know this isn't ideal and I genuinely appreciate your patience. Here's my offer: try the new filter + alert flow before the end of the month. If it doesn't work for your use case, reach out and I'll refund the prorated amount for this month — no questions asked.

Happy to answer any questions here or in a DM.

1+1=10 — 5/5/26, 9:36 AM
The docs are updated: https://www.rtpr.io/docs

Available fields:
title, body, title_or_body — the article text
ticker, tickers, tickers_length — the symbols mentioned
exchange — NASDAQ, NYSE, OTC, etc.
author — the wire source
created — publish timestamp
session — market session at publish time

Common operators:
contains, keyword — substring vs. whole-word match
starts_with, ends_with, equals
in, not_in — membership in a list
gt, gte, lt, lte — numeric comparisons
proximity — terms near each other
numeric_match — currency / percentage / count thresholds
